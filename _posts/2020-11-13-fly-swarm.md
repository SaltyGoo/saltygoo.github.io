title: Fly Swarm

R: 60'  D: [sum] rounds

Create a 30' cloud of flies. In it, actions that rely on sight are made with disadvantage. Wounded living creatures must make a save every round they spend in it or catch [Rat-Bite Fever](/monsters/rat).
