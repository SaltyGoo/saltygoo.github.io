title: Ghost Orchestra
---
**R**: 30'  **D**: [dice] x 10 minutes

You are surrounded by the harmonious sounds of an invisible eerie orchestra for the duration of the spell. All of your actions are now heightened by its music. Until it ends, creatures have a harder time suceeding on their saving throws against you.

Also, if you hide, the music stops, only to burst into a dramatic loud jump scare when you reveal yourself, [stunning](/2020/11/09/base-rules/) surprised creatures. 

Alternatively, you can cast this spell on another creature, preventing it from doing anything discreet for the duration.
