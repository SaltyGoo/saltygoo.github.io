**R**: Touch

Your touch causes the chaotic workings of life to permeate nonliving matter, up to approximately a [dice] x 5’ cube at a time.

The process takes 3 turns during which contact connot be broken. The first two turns of contact lead to softening and weakness, as veins, entrails, and other organic appurtenances metastasize, and after 3 turns, the matter collapses entirely into warm, pulsing slime.

Alternatively, if the target is a creature, it must save or [mutate](https://coinsandscrolls.blogspot.com/2018/01/osr-1d500-biological-mutations.html).
