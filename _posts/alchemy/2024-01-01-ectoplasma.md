title: Ectoplasm
---

Physical emotional matter that forms a ghost. If mixed with a potion or crafting materials, the referee will add ‘_ghostly_’ to one of its effects or attribute. You can discuss with them how you would interpret that.

If ingested, you can speak with the dead for 10 minutes.

##### Harvested from a [Ghost](/monsters/ghost) or other ethereal undeads
