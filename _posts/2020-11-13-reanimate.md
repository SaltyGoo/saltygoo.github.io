**R**: Touch

Slap your hands on the chest of a living creature that dies in the last [dice] turns. Roll 1D6. The target:
- 1-2. Nothing happens.
- 3-4. Gains [dice] Hit Points.
- 5-6. Gains [sum] Hit Points.
