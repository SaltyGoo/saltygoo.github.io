**R**: 120’

Your maleficence is unique and reflects your own essence: It’s an outpour of magic that inflicts [sum]+[dice] damage to one target. When you first choose this spell, decide or roll which elemental shape it has. You might find different types of Maleficences during your adventures and learn them.
