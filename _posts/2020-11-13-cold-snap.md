title: Cold Snap
---

**R:** 30' **D:** [dice] * 10 minutes

You send all water spirits into a sudden torpor, freezing all exposed water up to 1' deep. All bodies of water frozen likeso will be extremely angry once thawed.

If you invest 4 or more dice in this spell, the range is the whole hex you are in.
