**R**: Touch  **D**: [dice] nights

You spend 1 night draining the blood of a slain victim. For the duration, each member of its bloodline, wether through family, marriage, or covenant, is [charmed](/2020/11/09/base-rules/) by you.

If you invest 4 or more dice in this spell, the duration becomes permanent.
