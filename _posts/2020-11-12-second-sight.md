title: Second Sight
---
**D**: [dice] x 10 minutes

To the second sight, spellcasters radiate the presence of their known spells and enchanted items crackle with energy. Enchantments reveal [dice] aspects of their nature and invisible and ethereal things can be seen.

You can invest 2 extra [dice] into this spell and make it permanent. Doing this costs an inventory slot.
