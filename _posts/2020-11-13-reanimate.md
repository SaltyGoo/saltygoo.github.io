**R**: Touch

Slap your hands on the chest of a fallen creature. Roll 1D6. The target:
- 1-2. Gains a [Scar](/2020/11/09/base-rules/).
- 3-4. Gains [dice] Hit Points.
- 5-6. Gains [sum] Hit Points.
