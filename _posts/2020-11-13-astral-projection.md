title: Astral Projection
---
**D**: [dice] x 10 minutes

Your spirit is liberated and may venture safely up to [dice]*10’ from your body (which remains in stasis while the spirit is absent). The spirit is ethereal (and thus invisible to most mortal creatures), and may pass through a thickness of rock equal to [dice] feet, but is barred by lead or magical wards.

While ethereal, you may reach into the brains of others to raid surface thoughts, though you must succeed on a spell save to do so on targets of higher HD than [dice].
