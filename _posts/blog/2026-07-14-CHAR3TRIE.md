Hello!
This is the third edition of Charcuterie Board, the GLoG discord community zine. The first edition was published [online](https://oblidisideryptch.blogspot.com/2020/12/charcuterie-board-glog-zine.html) in 2020, and the second edition was never published. This zine is not even about the GLoG. It's about a wide and warm river, the Jabberwock, lazily winding its way through weird locales and sheltering even weirder creatures.

I'm very proud of this collective work that has been a year in the making, if not more! A zine from the GLoG discord channel with contributions by a plethora of amazing rpg bloggers and artists with graphic design by me. It's OSR compatible, and entirely DM facing. It will even be printed at some point! I'm too overwhelmed to write more, just read it!

**DUNGEONS - RIVER GENERATORS - MONSTERS - RIVER FREAKS - REED OBJECTS - ALCHEMICAL SYSTEM - BOAT MAKING TUTORIALS**
